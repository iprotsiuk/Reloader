# Hunting & Competitions Design

> **Prerequisites:** Read [core-architecture.md](core-architecture.md) first.
> **Status:** Deferred side systems. They are no longer the top-level progression spine for the project.

---

## Direction [v1+]

Hunting and competitions remain optional future side activities.

They may return later if they strengthen the core precision-contract sandbox by:
- giving the player lower-risk ways to test rifles and loads
- providing optional reputation or money on the side
- expanding the broader gun culture of the town

They must not displace assassination contracts as the primary economy and progression loop.

---

## Design Constraint [v1+]

If these systems are implemented later:
- they should reuse the same precision-shooting stack as contract gameplay
- they should stay compatible with police heat, inventory, save/load, and world travel
- their rewards should remain secondary to premium contract payouts

Use [assassination-contracts.md](assassination-contracts.md) for the current top-level fantasy.
